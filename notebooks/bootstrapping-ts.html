Bootstrapping Time Series

Description of the problem.

  • Methods based on a Markov approximation:

    • U. Lall and A. Sharma, "A Nearest Neighbor Bootstrap For Resampling Hydrologic Time Series," Water Resources Research, vol. 32, no. 3, pp. 679–693, Mar. 1996.
    • F. Paparella, A. Provenzale, L. A. Smith, C. Taricco, and R. Vio, "Local random analogue prediction of nonlinear processes," Physics Letters A, vol. 235, no. 3, pp. 233–240, 1997.
    • L. Pan and D. N. Politis, "Bootstrap prediction intervals for Markov processes," Computational Statistics & Data Analysis, vol. 100, pp. 467–494, 2016.
    • M. B. Rajarshi, "Bootstrap in Markov-sequences based on estimates of transition density," Annals of the Institute of Statistical Mathematics, vol. 42, no. 2, pp. 253–268, 1990.

  • Methods based on Autoregressive approximation:

    • L. Pan, Bootstrap prediction intervals for time series. PhD thesis. 2013.

  • Ideas from the Nonlinear Dynamics Community:

    • M. Thiel, M. C. Romano, J. Kurths, M. Rolfs, and R. Kliegl, "Twin surrogates to test for complex synchronisation," Europhys. Lett., vol. 75, no. 4, pp. 535–541, Aug. 2006.

  • Bootstrapping Information Theoretic Quantities:

    • Y. Gao, I. Kontoyiannis, and E. Bienenstock, "Estimating the entropy of binary time series: Methodology, some theory and a simulation study," arXiv.org, vol. cs.IT, no. 2., pp. 71–99, 29-Feb-2008. preprint
