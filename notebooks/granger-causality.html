Granger Causality

  • The impact of filtering on Granger Causality:

    • E. Florin, J. Gross, J. Pfeifer, G. R. Fink, and L. Timmermann, "The effect of filtering on Granger causality based multivariate causality measures," NeuroImage, vol. 50, no. 2, pp. 577-588, Apr. 2010.

    • L. Barnett and A. K. Seth, "Behaviour of Granger causality under filtering: Theoretical invariance and practical application," Journal of Neuroscience Methods, vol. 201, no. 2, pp. 404-419, Oct. 2011.

  • Problems with Granger Causality, as inheritted from Transfer Entropy:

    • D. A. Smirnov, "Spurious causalities with transfer entropy," Phys. Rev. E, vol. 87, no. 4, p. 042917, Apr. 2013.
