Notebooks

A collection of notebooks. The idea for which was stolen more-or-less out of whole cloth from Cosma Shalizi's excellent collection of notebooks. These serve as placeholders as I grope my way to understanding of various topics relevant to my academic research. At present, mostly a way for me to answer the question, "What was that paper about that thing again?" in a more rational way.

The entries are listed in order of most-recently edited. So they also act as snapshots in time of what I'm currently thinking about.
